The Taste of
Alexandria, Right Here

Born from a love of Alexandrian coastal cuisine, Seagull brings the vibrant flavors of Egypt's Mediterranean shore to Paterson, New Jersey. Every dish — from our signature grilled branzino to our hand-seasoned lamb chops — is made to transport you.
