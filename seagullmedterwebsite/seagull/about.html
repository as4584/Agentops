Who We Are

A Taste of the
Mediterranean Shore

From Alexandria's famous seafood markets to the streets of Paterson, New Jersey — our story is about bringing something real to the table.

Seagull Mediterranean Seafood was born from a deep love of Alexandrian cuisine — the bold, fresh, coastal flavors of Egypt's northern shore, where Mediterranean seafood has been celebrated for centuries. We brought that tradition to Paterson, NJ, and made it our home.

Everything at Seagull starts with freshness. Our fish tank showcases the day's catch, ensuring that every dish that leaves our kitchen is made with the finest ingredients. From the moment you walk in, you can see, smell, and taste the difference.

Our menu draws from the rich culinary heritage of Alexandria — charcoal-grilled fish with aromatic spices, hand-seasoned lamb, buttery scallops, and the freshest mezze — all served with the warmth and hospitality that defines Egyptian culture.

We are proud to be 100% halal certified, creating a welcoming table for the entire Paterson community and beyond. Whether you're a first-time guest or a regular who knows your table, you'll always be treated like family here.
